Fukuoka AI News

An AI-powered local news service that creates daily video summaries of Fukuoka area news for TikTok.

What We Do

Fukuoka AI News automatically collects, summarizes, and creates short-form video content about local news in the Fukuoka area, Japan. All content is generated using artificial intelligence and is clearly labeled as AI-generated.

How It Works

  1. Local news is collected via web search every morning
  2. An AI generates a video script based on the selected news
  3. A human reviews and approves the script before any video is created
  4. AI generates illustration-style video with narration
  5. The approved video is posted to TikTok with AI disclosure labels

Important Notices

  • All videos are clearly labeled as AI-generated content
  • Every video includes a disclaimer: "This is an AI recreation and not actual footage"
  • No real individuals' faces are depicted in AI-generated imagery
  • A human reviews and approves all content before publication
