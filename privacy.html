Japan Network Group Inc. ("we", "us", "our") operates the Fukuoka AI News service ("Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information in connection with the Service, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Overview

Fukuoka AI News is an automated content creation service that generates and publishes AI-created local news video summaries for the Fukuoka area on TikTok. The Service uses the TikTok Content Posting API and TikTok Login Kit solely for publishing videos to TikTok accounts authorized by the operator.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 TikTok API Data

When authentication with TikTok is performed, the following data is obtained through the TikTok API:

FieldSource / ScopePurpose
access_token OAuth 2.0 (Login Kit) Authenticate API requests to publish videos
refresh_token OAuth 2.0 (Login Kit) Refresh the access token when expired
open_id OAuth 2.0 (Login Kit) Identify the TikTok account authorized for posting
user.info.basic (display name, avatar URL, union_id) user.info.basic scope Display the connected account on the operator's dashboard
video.upload / video.publish Content Posting API Upload and publish AI-generated videos to the operator's own TikTok account

No additional TikTok data (followers, analytics beyond what is needed to verify publication, DMs, comments) is collected.

2.2 Other Data

  • Publicly available news data — collected from public news sources for AI content creation. This does not include personal data.

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal data from TikTok viewers or the general public
  • Cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics on this website
  • Browsing behavior or device fingerprints
  • Contact information from viewers
  • Location data beyond publicly available news context

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom, we process personal data on the following legal bases under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — when you authorize the Service via TikTok Login Kit, you provide explicit consent for us to access and use the listed API data
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — to provide the Service you have requested
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — to operate, secure, and improve the Service, provided such interests are not overridden by your rights

4. How We Use Information

  • Authentication data is used solely to authenticate with TikTok and publish videos via the Content Posting API
  • News data is used to generate AI video scripts and content
  • We do not use collected data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making with legal effects
  • We do not sell or rent any personal information

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties. Data may be transmitted to the following processors strictly for service operation:

  • TikTok / ByteDance — video publication via the official Content Posting API
  • fal.ai — AI image and video generation (no personal data transmitted)
  • Anthropic — AI text generation (no personal data transmitted)

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Japan Network Group Inc., our users, or others.

6. International Data Transfers

Some of our processors (TikTok, fal.ai, Anthropic) are located outside Japan, including in the United States and Singapore. When data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the vendors' own compliance programs.

8. Data Security

API tokens and configuration data are stored locally on secured hardware operated by Japan Network Group Inc. with the following safeguards:

  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Full-disk encryption on storage devices
  • Tokens stored in environment variables or encrypted configuration files, never committed to source control
  • All API communications use HTTPS/TLS
  • No personal data is stored in public cloud services

10. How to Exercise Your Rights / Data Deletion

To exercise any of the rights above, including requesting deletion of data collected via the TikTok API, please contact us at k-senba@japannetwork.co.jp with:
