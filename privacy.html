Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2026

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

1. Overview

Fukuoka AI News ("the Service") is an automated content creation service that generates and publishes AI-created local news video summaries for the Fukuoka area on TikTok. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with our Service.

2. Information We Collect

The Service collects only the minimum data required to operate:

  • TikTok API authentication tokens: Used solely for publishing videos to our TikTok account
  • Publicly available news data: Collected from public news sources for content creation

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal data from TikTok viewers or users
  • Cookies or tracking information
  • User browsing behavior
  • Contact information from viewers

3. How We Use Information

  • Authentication data is used solely for posting videos to our TikTok account via the Content Posting API
  • News data is used to generate AI video scripts and content
  • TikTok analytics data (if accessed) is used only to improve our content quality

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties. The only data transmitted externally is:

  • Video content posted to TikTok via the official API
  • API requests to AI service providers (fal.ai, Anthropic) for content generation — no personal data is included

5. Data Storage and Security

API tokens and configuration data are stored locally on secured hardware. No personal data is stored in cloud services. Access is restricted to the Service operator.

6. AI-Generated Content

All video content is generated using artificial intelligence. Videos are clearly labeled as AI-generated content per TikTok's guidelines. AI recreations do not depict identifiable real individuals.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our content is intended for general audiences.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach out via our TikTok account or through the contact information provided on our main page.
